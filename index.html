Who We Are

Setting Standards in Cleaning Chemistry

Based in the industrial estate of Ramanayyapeta, Kakinada, Sri Tirumala Chemical Industries manufactures superior-grade disinfection, household, and industrial cleaning formulations.

Our focus is simple: high active matter, safe synthesis, and premium effectiveness. From water-chlorination bleaching powder to refreshing pine floor cleaners and grease-cutting dishwash gels, our TIRUMALA brand represents chemical integrity and reliability. We formulate custom strengths and handle bulk supply contracts for companies across Andhra Pradesh.
